THE VISION


The Vision of Project 160,000 Entrepreneurs is into 3 phases:

PHASE ONE: Creating an average of 10,000 micro or small scale entrepreneurs in each of the 16 local government areas of Ekiti State within the next 10 years (Before 31st December, 2030)
[i.e. 10,000 X 16 L.G.A. = 160,000 Entrepreneurs]


PHASE TWO: Creating at least 1,600 small or medium scale entrepreneurs in the FCT and an average of 4,400 small or medium scale entrepreneurs in each of the 36 states of Nigeria within the next 15 years (Before 31st December, 2035)
[i.e. 1,600 + (4,400 X 36 States) = 160,000 Entrepreneurs]


PHASE THREE: Creating at least 11,600 medium or large scale entrepreneurs in Nigeria and an average of 2,800 medium or large scale entrepreneurs in each of the remaining 53 countries in Africa within the next 20 years (Before 31st December, 2040)
[i.e. 11,600 + (2,800 X 53 Countries) = 160,000 Entrepreneurs]


THE MISSION

Our mission at Project 160,000 Entrepreneurs is to provide quality intensive practical training on Entrepreneurship, Improved Agricultural, ICT, Basic Manufacturing or any other Vocational courses for all our enrolled members as we provide the links to start-up capital, expansion capital and quality business mentoring for them.
