DEMOCRATIZING JOB CREATION

Project 160,000 Entrepreneurs, (P160Ke) exists simply to democratize job creation in Africa. Our commitment compliments the efforts of Governments in Africa in the area of job creation. We are strategically set to create 1.6 million jobs by empowering 160,000 entrepreneurs who will create an average of 10 jobs each, by 2030.

LEARN MORE

OBJECTIVES

Economic Empowerment

Identify, mobilize, and position Africa’s youths to be economically empowered and self-sustainable.

Economic Growth

Increase and support active youth participation in economic activities towards economic growth of the Continent.

Economic Development

Foster linkages and partnership with key stakeholders of Africa’s economic development.

GET TO KNOW ABOUT OUR:

  • MISSION

    Our mission at Project 160,000 Entrepreneurs is to provide quality intensive practical training on Entrepreneurship, Improved Agricultural, ICT, Basic Manufacturing or any other Vocational courses for all our enrolled members as we provide the links to start-up capital, expansion capital and quality business mentoring for them.

  • VISION

    To contribute significantly to sustainable job creation in Africa, by creating at least 160,000 entrepreneurs.
